Phishing Awareness & Prevention

What is Phishing?

Phishing is a cyber attack that tricks users into revealing sensitive information, such as passwords and financial details, by pretending to be a trustworthy entity.

Common Types of Phishing Attacks

  • Email Phishing
  • Spear Phishing
  • Vishing (Voice Phishing)
  • Smishing (SMS Phishing)
  • Clone Phishing

How to Prevent Phishing?

1. Always verify the sender's email address.
2. Do not click on suspicious links.
3. Use multi-factor authentication.
4. Keep your software and antivirus updated.
5. Educate yourself and others about phishing threats.

Report Phishing Attempts

If you suspect a phishing attempt, report it to your IT department or cybersecurity team.
